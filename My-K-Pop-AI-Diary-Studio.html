🔒 Stay Safe, Stay Smart
A little reminder before you explore the world of AI 💜
🛡️

Your Diary is Personal. Use AI Wisely.

AI is an amazing creative tool — but it is important to stay safe online. Here is what to always remember:

  • 🚫 Never share your passwords, home address, phone number, or school name with any AI tool.
  • 🚫 Do not enter full names of real people in sensitive situations.
  • 🚫 Never share private family information or financial details.
  • ✅ Use AI for creativity, learning, journaling ideas, studying, and fun.
  • ✅ If something online feels weird or wrong, tell a trusted adult immediately.
  • ✅ Remember: AI does not know you personally — it is a very smart creative assistant, not a friend or counsellor.
  • 💜 Your thoughts and feelings are precious. Use AI to express them beautifully — but real support always comes from real people who love you.

✨ AI is a tool for your creativity, confidence & growth. You are always in control. Use it wisely, and it will help you become the best version of yourself! 💜
